      Add a Church

      You do not have any active churches in your profile. This is most likely because you have just registered for the first time.

      To begin, you need to connect your profile to at least one church or ministry. Click the button below to find the church you want to add to your profile.

      Search...

      Enter the name of the church you are looking for. You can enter part of the name for example "His" to search for all "His People" churches

      From the results, click the "check" icon. This will activate the church in your profile

      The church will now appear in the list of available database to log into. Simply click on church name to sign in

      Missing?

      If you are not able to find the church you are looking for, its most likely because they are not registered as a church on the godochurch online systems. If this is the case please choose from the following options:

      1. Please ask your church to consider registering with us. Send them to our website at www.godochurch.com
      2. Send us an email at support@gododata.com, and we will contact them for you.

      There are two reasons why the church or ministry does not appear in the list provided:

      1. They may not have an active database.
      2. Their database has not been activated in this application

      Either way please contact your church/ministry office to have this corrected.
